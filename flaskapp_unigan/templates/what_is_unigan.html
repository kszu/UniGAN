What is UniGAN?

Generate unisex product designs in seconds

1. Select shoe design

2. UniGAN-ify it

3. Review your output

Try three different UniGAN models to generate new shoe designs!

Use pre-selected sample images—or upload your own shoe designs via URL or file upload—to create new shoe designs with any of UniGAN's three design models.

Try it!

Gender

Transform your original shoe design into male, female, and unisex designs, as well as UniGAN's reconstruction of your design.

Attribute Intensity

Generate nine unique shoe designs of increasing intensity based on a given attribute that you select (including gender and shoe style categories).

Shoe Style Category

Recreate your footwear design in one of five shoe style categories: ankle, athletic, boots, flats, and heels.

UniGAN was created to solve two problems:

1) Consumers want to express their gender however they see fit, not to be shackled by traditional gender norms.
2) Corporations produce millions of tons of clothing waste each year, driven by many SKUâ€™s and product lines.

Enter UniGAN, the world's first unisex design generator

Built specifically for footwear designers, UniGAN leverages machine learning technology to combat gender bias at the origin of product development. Inclusivity is a win-win for consumers and businesses alike. Consumers express their gender as they see fit; brands reduce inventory and corporate waste.

UniGAN hopes to contribute to a larger effort to make consumer products inclusive and accessible first and foremost. By beginning with unisex design, footwear designers will help decrease toxic gender norms while also fighting corporate waste.
