What is UniGAN?

Generate unisex product designs in seconds

UniGAN was built in 2020 to solve two problems:

1) Consumers want traditionally-gendered products to reflect the gender fluid world that they now live in.
2) Corporations produce millions of tons of clothing waste each year, driven by many SKUâ€™s and product lines.

Enter UniGAN, the world's first unisex design generating product.

Built for product designers and developers, UniGAN leverages cutting edge machine learning technology to combat gender bias at the origin of product development. Inclusivity is a win-win for consumers and corporations alike. Consumers express their gender as they see fit; companies reduce inventory and corporate waste.

UniGAN is part of a larger effort to make consumer products inclusive and accessible first and foremost. By beginning with unisex design, product developers will help decrease toxic gender norms while also fighting corporate waste.
